Lembaga Sertifikasi Profesi

Professional Certification Organization

Bank Mandiri melalui Surat Keputusan Direksi Nomor KEP.DIR/10A/2023 tanggal 6 Maret 2023 telah memberikan persetujuan atas pendirian Lembaga Sertifikasi Profesi (LSP) Bank Mandiri. Pendirian LSP Bank Mandiri tersebut adalah untuk memastikan bahwa pegawai Bank Mandiri yang telah mendapatkan pembelajaran, pelatihan maupun pengalaman kerja, memiliki kompetensi (keahlian) yang diakui secara nasional dan dibuktikan dengan sertifikasi profesi yang telah memenuhi standar sertifikasi nasional yang diterbitkan oleh Lembaga Sertifikasi Bank Mandiri.

Bank Mandiri through Board of Directors Decree Number KEP.DIR/10A/2023 dated March 6, 2023. has approved the establishment of the Bank Mandiri Professional Certification Institute (LSP). Mandiri. The establishment of LSP Bank Mandiri is to ensure that employees of Bank Mandiri who have received learning, training and work experience, have a professional certification. employees who have received learning, training and work experience, have nationally recognized competencies (expertise). competence (expertise) that is nationally recognized and evidenced by professional certification that meets national certification standards. certification that meets national certification standards issued by the Bank Mandiri Certification Institute. Bank Mandiri Certification Institute.
